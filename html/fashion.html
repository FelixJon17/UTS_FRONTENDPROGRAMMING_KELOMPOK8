Casual Style

Casual style refers to a relaxed and comfortable approach to dressing that is suitable for informal settings. It blends comfort with practicality while still allowing individuals to express their personal style.

Skena Style

Skena Style can be defined as a fashion trend characterized by its unique identity. This style is often influenced by various musical genres, particularly those that are less mainstream.

Trendy Style

Here's a brief explanation of "Trendy Style" in English: Trendy Style refers to fashion that is in vogue and follows the latest trends and popular styles. It is characterized by its ever-changing nature, as what is considered "trendy" can shift rapidly based on current events.

Vintage Style

Vintage style refers to a fashion aesthetic that draws inspiration from past decades, typically from the 1920s to the 1990s. This style celebrates classic designs, quality craftsmanship, and timeless elegance.

Sporty Style

Athleisure is a fashion trend that combines athletic wear with casual, everyday clothing. It is designed for comfort and functionality while maintaining a stylish appearance.
